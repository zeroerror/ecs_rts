LowPoly Police Vehicles


This package contain Car, Van, Helicopter and Boat police Vehicles with very lowpoly models for mobile games.

All models are available in .fbx format in "Models" folder.

All models are using same texture placed in "Texture" folder.

All prefabs are placed in "Prefab" folder.

Vehicle prefabs contain animations, particles and sound effects which you can check preview by running "SampleScene".
